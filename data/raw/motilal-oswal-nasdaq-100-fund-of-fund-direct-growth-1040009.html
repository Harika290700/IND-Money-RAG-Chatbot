About Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF

Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF is an equity fund. This fund was started on 29 October, 2021. The fund is managed by Priya Sridhar. The fund could potentially beat inflation in the long-run.

Key Parameters

  1. Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF has ₹224 Cr worth of assets under management (AUM) as on Feb 2026 and is more than category average.
  2. The fund has an expense ratio 0.2.

Returns

Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF has given a CAGR return of -1.24% since inception. Over the last 1 and 3 years the fund has given a CAGR return of -18.97% and 1.96% respectively.

Holdings

Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF has allocated its funds majorly in Tech, Cash Equivalent. Its top holdings are Infosys Ltd, Tata Consultancy Services Ltd, HCL Technologies Ltd, Tech Mahindra Ltd, Wipro Ltd

Taxation

As it is a sector - technology mutual fund the taxation is as follows:
For short term (less than a year) capital gains will be taxed at 20%
For long term (more than 1 year) capital gains will be taxed at 12.5% without indexation benefit
Dividends will always be taxed at slab rate. Long term gains upto Rs 1 lakh are exempt capital gains tax.

Investment objective of Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF

The investment objective of the scheme is to provide returns that before expenses, closely correspond to the total returns of securities as represented by Nifty Information Technology TRI, subject to tracking errors. However, the performance of scheme may differ from that of the underlying index due to tracking error. The Scheme does not guarantee/indicate any returns. There can be no assurance that the schemes’ objectives will be achieved

Minimum Investment and lockin period

Minimum investment for lump sum payment is INR 0.00 and for SIP is INR 0.00. Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty IT ETF has no lock in period.
